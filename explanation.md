# PROMPT ENGINEERING EXPLANATION 
## Objective
To generate high-converting UGC-style advertisement content for an online coding course.
## Prompt Strategy
1. Role-Based Framing
The AI was instructed to act as a "UGC content creator" to simulate natural, authentic ad tone.

2. Clear Structure
The script was structured as:
Hook → Problem → Solution → Social Proof → CTA

This ensures persuasive flow.

3. Audience Definition
Specifying beginners and career switchers helped tailor messaging around fear, confusion, and career growth.

4. Platform Adaptation
Separate prompts were used for Instagram and YouTube Shorts to match tone and energy.

5. Conversion Focus
Hooks and CTA sections were generated separately to improve engagement and click-through rates.
## Outcome
The generated scripts:
- Feel natural and relatable
- Address career pain points
- Emphasize results and transformation
- Encourage immediate action
